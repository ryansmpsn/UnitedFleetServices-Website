Hello. We are United Fleet Services.

United Fleet Services, LLC (UFS) delivers surface logistics services and professional staff augmentation solutions to national clients. Our service advantage includes our specialty in servicing the federal government, state, and local municipalities.

UFS serves as an elite access point to provide operational and professional services with transportation delivery and supply & distribution management.

More About Us.

As a Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business well versed with government contract service demands, UFS is committed to excellence in performance and flexible innovated solutions for our clients.
